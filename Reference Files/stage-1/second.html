Create Lasting Bonds On Campus.

The college campus is home to thousands of students in their most important years. It is the first taste of complete freedom of expression they have ever had, we find the ability for students to feel comfortable on campus to be a pressing priority.

Our design specification

While the project is still in progress, and these designs might undergo a change, but our design emphasizes a more modern design that is familiar and intuitive to our users. We want our demographic to be able to easily interact with our application to fulfill a user need. The following is a wireframe to give an initial idea of what our project looks like:


Home Page

Home page to welcome students into the page, immediately calls students to the action of finding an RSO that fits them with the structure of the page. This page will also contain the searching algorithm that students can use to find RSOs.

Searching Algorithm

Searching mechanism that enables users to filter RSOs by interest, type of club, size, schedule, and even friends. This is powerful, as it allows students to shrink a huge campus to a small part. The searching algorithm is one of the most essential feature of the app.

Listing RSOs

This page lists the resulting RSOs, typically from a search initiated by a user, and it will show some initial information about the RSO, such as purpose and what a typical meeting entails. You can also get an idea of who is in the club from this page.


RSO Page

This page will contain all the information the user needs about the RSO, and includes interactions such as joining the RSO, talking to members, paying fees, checking calendar, etc.

User Profile

This application will contain its own social media that only students can use to get in touch with other students. This page contains the student name, picture, and ability to chat.

Messaging

As part of the social media, users can chat with each other, which is helpful for when students meet each other during an RSO session and would like to keep in touch. This also supports group chats within the RSO.
